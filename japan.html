japan

kyoto's nature

kyoto, a city in japan, was perfect! the fushimi inari taisah trail, in particular, was awesome to hike up. there are around 10,000 torii gates along that path!

osaka's streets at night

osaka, another city in japan, has streets that come alive at night.

kimonos

my family and i dressed up in kimonos and explored for a bit! it was so hot this day, so we were all overheating. major respect for the people who wore them every single day!

osaka castle

seeing the osaka castle and its grounds was breathtaking! it is beautifully constructed; architecture is awesome!

deer at nara park

the deer at nara park are so friendly! they will do almost anything for some food, including slobbering all over you. they will also always bow back to you in exchange for food.
