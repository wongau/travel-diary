hawai'i

waterfalls

hawai'i had soo many waterfalls! they were all beautiful, especially the ones along road to hana, a 64 mi long highway on the island of maui, with various stops along the way with beautiful hikes and views.

animals

it was amazing getting to see all the wildlife in hawai'i! seeing sea turtles up close was especially cool for me, as they are one of my favorite animals. we snorkeled alongside the seal, and it was so friendly and playful! it stuck around with our group for such a long time. the black clown fish's name, emo nemo, made me laugh.

black sand beach

the black sand beach on the road to hana was awesome! i've never seen black sand, remnants of volcanic rock, before.

sunsets

i did not experience one bad sunset while in hawai'i. the boolest one was the one we viewed at the top of haleakalā, the tallest mountin on the island of maui. the summit's elevation is 10,023 ft!

deer at nara park

the deer at nara park are so friendly! they will do almost anything for some food, including slobbering all over you. they will also always bow back to you in exchange for food.
